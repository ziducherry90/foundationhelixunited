Our Impact

Who We Are

In the heart of Bangladesh, a beacon of hope emerged in the form of the Foundation of Helix United. Inspired by the spirit of the Helix United Football Club, we are a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering youth and addressing societal issues.

We are proud to align our mission with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Through the universal language of football, we actively work towards No Poverty, ensuring Good Health & Well-being, providing Quality Education through discipline, and Reducing Inequalities in our community.

Join us as we harness the power of teamwork to build a legacy of unity, happiness, and a brighter, sustainable future for the next generation.

Our Goals

Spreading Joy

Using the universal language of football to transcend boundaries, bring people together, and foster a love for the beautiful game.

Social Impact

Devising effective solutions for education, healthcare, and social equality to drive meaningful change in society.

Youth Empowerment

Sustainable Development Goals

We are committed to the United Nations SDGs to create a better world.

No Poverty

Supporting the underprivileged through winter drives, Ramadan food distribution, and charity events.

Good Health

Promoting physical and mental well-being through football and active community sports.

Quality Education

Empowering youth with discipline, leadership skills, and teamwork through organized sports.

Reduced Inequalities

Bridging gaps in society by bringing people together regardless of background through unity.
